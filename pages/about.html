About MyCareerMap

Helping learners make confident career decisions.

What is MyCareerMap?

MyCareerMap is a digital career guidance platform designed to help learners explore career options based on their school subjects, skills, and interests. The app connects education to real-world careers, making career planning clearer and more accessible.

What You Can Do

Explore Careers

Browse a wide range of careers across different fields such as IT, Engineering, Health, and Business.

Subject Matching

See how your school subjects link directly to different career paths and opportunities.

Career Insights

View key information such as required skills, salary ranges, demand in South Africa, and popular cities.

Future Planning

Discover universities and institutions that offer relevant qualifications for your chosen career path.

About the Developer

MyCareerMap was developed by a passionate full-stack development student Mr M, with a strong interest in building practical, user-friendly applications. The goal behind this project is to use technology to solve real problems faced by learners when choosing careers.

This application was built using modern web technologies including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a strong focus on clean design, accessibility, and scalability.
